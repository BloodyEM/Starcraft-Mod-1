#name: Urthos III
"You can't make me go back there."
          Sgt. Matthew Murdoch - Veteran of 2 tours of duty on Urthos III 

The small, mineral rich moon of Urthos III was one of the mostly fiercely contested territories during the recent UED conflict. It changed hands so often (and in such a bloody fashion) that the area eventually became known simply as the Meatgrinder. Thousands died there in pitched battles that often involved multiple forces from each species. Although the battlefields there are now quiet, ambitious leaders in search of crystals to fuel their ravenous machines of war tend to forget the costs of holding such a place...
